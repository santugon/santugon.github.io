Specific Plans of Action

BLAZE MASTERCLASS

The BLAZE Masterclass is an avenue for us COB students to better understand and develop our skills necessary for a COB student. From weekly workshops about resume building, handling legal business processes, and even building start-ups. This project will prepare us, BLAZE2024, to be the lasallian business leaders of tomorrow.

COBoto Na!

COBoto na! is a video series blast for the HALALAN2022 elections that aims to educate BLAZE2024 on the importance of voters education as newly registered voters. The video series will focus on introducing the presidential, vice presidential, and senatorial candidates and their previous impacts on the business sectors.

COBasics: A Survival Guide Series

The BLAZE2024 Survival Guide Series aims to be a year-long initiative centered on maximizing one's student life by offering various student services and batch activities that focuses on providing internal and external aid in the university to each and every student to become a more unified BLAZE2024. This includes projects such as Platform tutorials, newsletters, Announcement hubs, and more.

BLAZE Unplugged

BLAZE Unplugged is a series of interactive projects such as game nights, block tournamtes, block hurras, and the like. This project focuses on providing students a chance to "escape" from academics and be an avenue where they can freely showcase their skills, talents, while at the same time promoting friendship and unity.

Hawak Kamay

Hawak Kamay is a fundrasing project that will be a bridge for BLAZE2024 to work together hand-in-hand and show our collaborative work not only to our Lasallian Community as the beneficiary, but also to our fellow Filipinos.

Dear BLAZE2024

DEAR BLAZE2024, is a video series for the batch from various COB alumni' that aims to show an in depth understanding of their individual courses to show their life may be after college and what is in store for them. It serves as a personal avenue to hear and understand the journey and life of a Lasallian Business Leader outside the university.
